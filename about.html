About Us

Kolawole k. Salami (born 2nd Feb 1987) is a Nigerian entrepreneur who is currently the Country Director of Cristabel Resource & Investment Nig, since 2019.

Cristabel Resource & Investment Nig is one of the leading logistic companies present in three Africa countries (Nigeria,Kenya and Ethiopia). focuses on providing complex logistic solutions with the utmost consideration for the environment. In 2019, Cristabel Resource & Investment Nig introduced the “Green 2PL Solutions” logistic product – a product that connects complex logistics (2PL) with minimal environmental impact. In addition to complex logistic solutions Cristabell Resource & Investment Nig. Ltd also provides individual logistic services, namely transport solutions, storage, distribution logistics, outsourcing solutions, FMCG sale and value-added services (VAS). The key aim of Cristabel Resource & Investment Nig is to establish and maintain a long-term relationship with our customers. This fact is reflected in the above-mentioned corporate motto: „Our team for your efficiency” always placing an emphasis on efficient and top-ranking logistic services. The company prepares tailor-made solutions for each customer and their needs. Therefore, among other things, extraordinary attention is paid to innovation and continuous development of technologies and processes.
